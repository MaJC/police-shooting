Legend

This map uses circles to show areas across the United States where there has been a police-involved shooting. More information about the victims are displayed below once a circle on the map has been clicked.

  • Blue circle denotes a male victim
  • Pink circle denotes a female victim
  • Yellow circle denotes that the victim's gender was not reported or is unknown
  • Purple circle denotes that there were both male and female victims in the area
  • A bigger circle denotes an older victim
  • The upper right corner on the map can be used to change the map's layers or to display certain victims depending on whether they were killed or hit.

Zoom in and click on a circle to see more information about the incident plus the victim.
